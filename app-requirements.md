# Personal Improvement App — Requirements

## Core Idea
How do we take you from Reality to Expectation in least time, with steps broken down for each goal, tracking them & showing — with each step — that you're getting closer to the goal.

## Problem Statement
Our Hero (Me & you) is trying to improve but doesn't have a proper tool to track & progress.

---

## Reality vs Expectation (Example User Profile)

| Area | Reality | Expectation |
|---|---|---|
| Fitness | >25% body fat | <15% body fat |
| Earning | 42k/lakhs | 1 cr + multiple streams |
| Car | No car, looking for second-hand under 7-8 lakhs | A car worth 25/50 lakhs, no issue, easy process |
| Home | Yes, but was 50 lakhs | Multiple flats, plots, investment >3 cr |
| Skills | Salesforce dev & architectural coding + learn AI | Not sticking to one skill, Public speaker, Can deep work >5hrs, Tutor, Sales, Builder, Enterprise membership |
| Lifestyle | No foreign trips | Multiple foreign trips for business and leisure, Business first class travel |
| Happiness | Low | Satisfied with self |
| Satisfaction | Even lower | Value added to society |

## Features

### Goal Tracking
- Goals broken down into steps (like a JIRA board)
- Steps can be moved to: To Do / In Progress / Done / Blocked
- Show progress % against each goal
- Dashboard to see everything at once

### Gamification
- With each step completed → Game mechanic triggered
- Give points & Badge for added consistency
- Added points / Badges for consistency

### AI Integration
- Ask AI to break a goal into steps
- "Describe your issue" input → for context to AI

### Notifications & Reports
- Send weekly progress emails to user

## Sample Goals & Steps

### i) Earnings
1. Launch your course about what you know → SF development, lifestyle, projects etc.
2. Build your own product like this Goal Tracker
3. Upskill & get better job opportunities (30 lakhs+)

### ii) Fitness
1. Go to gym 3/5 times a week
2. Running/Cardio → Run 4 times a week
3. Eat less but better → high protein, low carb, sugar diet
- Result = Less than 15% body fat

### iii) Car
- Byproduct of Earnings goal

### iv) Lifestyle
- Being at a place that values you, will give freedom
- Investing time & money on business ideas = Give good results → Better lifestyle

### v) Happiness
- Do things that you say/write you'll do = Happiness

### vi) Satisfaction
- Automatic byproduct of all above

## Progress Visualization
- Day 30 view: Progress = 20%, Amount paid shown

## Hero Visualizer
- A dedicated visualizer screen showing the user's hero (avatar/stick figure) on a journey from Reality to Expectation
- Horizontal timeline from left (Reality) to right (Expectation)
- Hero avatar position on the timeline reflects overall progress across all goals
- Left side shows the user's Reality state for each goal (current values)
- Right side shows the Expectation state for each goal (target values)
- Each goal listed with current value vs target value visible on screen
- As steps are completed, the hero moves closer to the Expectation end
- Visual indicator of how much has been achieved vs how much remains
